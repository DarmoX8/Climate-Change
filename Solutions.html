Solar Energy from Space

Name of Innovation - Cresolar

This concept involves capturing solar energy in space and transmitting it to Earth for use as a clean and virtually limitless energy source. Here's how it works:

  • Solar panels placed in orbit around the Earth collect sunlight nonstop, as there are no atmospheric or nighttime limitations in space.
  • This collected solar energy is converted into electricity in the body and then transmitted to the Earth's surface using the antenna.
  • On the ground, specialized receivers capture the transmitted energy and convert it back into electricity.

Aspects of this Innovation

  1. Abundant Energy:

    Space-based solar power offers a constant and vast source of renewable energy that is not subject to weather or time-of-day limitations, unlike ground-based solar or wind power.

  2. Stop Climate Change:

    By providing a significant portion of the world's energy needs through clean solar power, it can drastically reduce the reliance on fossil fuels and, therefore, help mitigate climate change. The potential impact on climate change is enormous.

  3. Zero Pollution:

    Unlike fossil fuel-based energy sources, which release harmful pollutants and greenhouse gases into the atmosphere when burned, space-based solar power generation is completely clean and does not emit any pollutants.
